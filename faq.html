Frequently Asked Questions

How often do we meet?

We meet at a minimum of once a month. You can read more than one book however and attend as many of the sessions as you like. For now there are a maximum of five sessions a month, but as our membership increases we expect the number of sessions to increase.

How do we catch up on missed sessions?

If you can't make it to one of our sessions you're free to discuss the book via our social media page on Facebook or Twitter, or you can subscribe to our monthly podcast on Youtube, where we will bring up some of the discussion points that we brought up during the session. Members may be invited onto the podcast to review the book that was covered.

Is there an age limit?

As the sessions are held in a bar, you must be at least 18 before you can join. However, if you are old enough to sign up to Facebook, Twitter, or Youtube, you are free to follow the discussion there until you are 18 years old.

How do we get the books we want to read?

The books we cover may be purchased from any local book store or online book retailer.

Is it okay if I can't complete the book?

Ideally you should complete the book, but sometimes circumstances don't allow for that. You're still free to discuss what you have read at the sessions if you are still interested in participating.

Why a book club?

Many of us love books but often bore the ear of our loved ones who aren't as enthusiatic about them as we are. Our mission is to bring together like minded individuals who are interested in the long dissection of a book after its completion.

How long is each session?

The private lounge is exclusive to our club for only 90 minutes, so the sessions will typically fill that time. You are still free to remain in the bar with any other members who decide to stay and continue discussing whatever you like.

How is each book picked?

At the end of the session, each member will share some books they would like to cover at the next session and then a book is voted on.
